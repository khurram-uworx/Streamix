# Business Goal

## Company

Streamix Intelligence — a developer-tooling company building reactive data
pipelines for .NET. The flagship product is a streaming framework with first-class
support for AI/data-engineering workloads (embeddings, vector search, LLM
orchestration) on top of `IAsyncEnumerable`.

## Target market

Backend engineers, platform teams, and AI/data engineers building event-driven
systems on .NET. Particular interest in the convergence of streaming pipelines,
vector databases, and LLM orchestration.

## Objective

Track external signals that materially affect product strategy, customer
demand, or competitive positioning: announcements about AI/ML tooling and
models, cloud-infrastructure shifts (managed compute, serverless, AI-native
services), developer-tool releases (especially .NET, language runtimes,
package managers, IDE/CLI tooling), security incidents and advisories that
affect the .NET ecosystem or popular dependencies, open-source movements
(license changes, governance, notable releases), and emerging industry
regulation around AI and data. Filter out unrelated tech news.
